Nexus*

The project*

Nexus is a coliving experience for the curious and passionate.

For 5 weeks in the summer, you'll join a community of around 10 students, entrepreneurs, hackers, and builders.

You'll join us in rural California, where you'll be able to pursue your work, studies, research or creative projects surrounded by nature and likeminded people.
